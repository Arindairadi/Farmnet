Growing Together

FarmNet is revolutionizing agriculture in Uganda through technology, education, and sustainable practices.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, FarmNet began as a small initiative to connect local farmers in Kabale with better markets. Today, we've grown into Uganda's leading agricultural innovation platform.

Our Humble Beginnings

Started with just 5 farmers in Kabale district, we've grown to serve over 10,000 farmers across Uganda. Our journey has been one of perseverance and community.

Technology Meets Agriculture

We bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern technology, providing farmers with tools to increase yields and connect with markets.

Community Focus

At our core, we're about people. Our programs are designed to uplift entire communities through sustainable agricultural practices.
